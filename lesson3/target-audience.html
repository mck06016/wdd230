Target Audience

Target Audience

The target audience extends from business owners to visitors to our city. Information on businesses to expand and network with each other as well as connect with a wide range of customers and other enterprises. The site will be populated by information on local business owners and entrepreneurs to network and expand their business through collaboration. The site will also be available for consumers to research and obtain valuable information for businesses located in Chilliwack and the surrounding areas.

Persona: Business Owner

Name: Mr. David Peterson

David is 35 years old, single male with 2 kids. He went to school and studied both Marketing and business management. He opened a market with a variety of products ranging from low price to very high end. He markets items across a variety of platforms and advertises that he has something for everyone’s needs and budget. He is looking to connect with other businesses in the area to offer complementary promotions and to cross market with other businesses.

Persona: Customer

Name: Hannah Stevens

Hannah is 22 years old and engaged. She prides herself on looking for high quality products and reviewing and comparing her options to both get the best deal and ensure the quality of the items she is shopping for. She will also provide both good and bad honest reviews of businesses based on the service and quality of the products. She also shares her experiences on multiple media platforms, so can be a source of good or bad advertising based on her experience.

First Scenario:

The user is looking for a selection of businesses that offer a specific service. They navigate to the site from a mobile device over the course of 2-3 days and then go to individual business sites linked through the chamber of commerce site.

Second Scenario:

The user is a business manager looking at alternate and competing businesses current advertising and marketing strategies. Most review of the site is from a desktop computer during business hours. They return to the site every 2-4 weeks for reviews of new businesses.

Third Scenario:

The user is starting a new business, has registered their business with the city, but has not yet reached out to the chamber of commerce. They are reviewing the site to see the value that the site has for new or established businesses. They are specifically looking at the benefits associated with membership in the chamber of commerce. This user is generally going to connect via a desktop or laptop computer.

Fourth Scenario:

The user is a young professional looking for a job that needs to be close to home. They will generally connect with mobile devices. Although also using job sites to search for employment, they want to ensure they reach out to businesses that are specific to their field of study even if they are not currently hiring. They want to tailor a resume and cover letter to deliver and be kept on hand until they are hiring next.

Fifth Scenario:

This user is doing a business search and has linked to the chamber of commerce site from their mobile device. They are looking for 2-3 different businesses to get a quote for a specific service.
